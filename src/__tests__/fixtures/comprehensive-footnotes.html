sn This Aramaic word is found three times in the New Testament (Mark 14:36; Rom 8:15; Gal 4:6). It was probably one of the words that Jesus himself used, and it expresses the believer's relationship to God as Father.

4 tc The translation assumes that the form translated "beginning" is in the absolute state rather than the construct ("in the beginning of," or "when God created").
